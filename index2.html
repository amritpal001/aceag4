Arts and Crafts on Environmental Awareness

This workshop was made because it teaches us how to create plant pots on recyclable materials and it also aims to preserve/maintain a good environment.

Find Out More
What is this workshop all about?

This workshop is all about raising awareness of our environment through the help of arts and crafts using plastic waste material or other recyclable waste to lessen the waste that we produce, encouraging the participants and other professionals to engage to implement environmental awareness through the use of arts and crafts.

And, since there's a lot of waste being thrown out everyday (this includes plastics, tin cans, etc). You could essentially use Arts and Crafts to transform these materials to good use as pots with plants, props, and many more things! Here are some examples of this: Like this plastic bottle being turned into this prop. Or, this tincan being turned into this tin can pot with plants.

Importance and Benefits of this workshop:


  • • We help prolong the life of the material by making another use for the recycled materials.



  • • We can lessen the number of waste that are compiled to no end in a period of time.



  • • We can have small furnitures inside out eco friendly, plus anyone can sell it to small communities or donate it to eco friendly groups that are focused on environmental safety.

Tips & Facts:

First (I)

Lessen the purchase of tons of plastic water bottle when using a bottle can.

Second (II)

Don't take plastic straws in fast food chains, buy yourself a metal straw.

Third (III)

Keep newspapers and magazines, it can be used as a self improvised gift wrapper, paper mache, DIY decorations, less spending.

Fourth (IV)

Did you know: Arts and crafts boosts self confidence and strongly practicing this correlates with higher achievement in reading and maths.

Fifth (V)

Did you know: Art cannot be taught, it comes from thinking. But it can be improved by practice.
